Subsea intervention

  1. Home
  2. Upstream
  3. Subsea intervention

Browns Engineering offers the industry leading 7Series subsea intervention system, which can be used for services ranging from simple shifting of a sliding sleeve to performing a full plug and abandonment.

The 7Series is a pressure-containing system that allows well intervention operations to be carried out on subsea wells without compromising the dual-pressure containment boundaries.

Typical applications for the system include:

  • Slickline access into the well
  • Braided line access into the well
  • E-line access into the well
  • Pumping operations
The system is deployable from any vessel of opportunity and can work in water depths ranging from 100-ft. to 10,000-ft. The 7Series is capable of intervening on wells with shut-in pressures up to 10,000 psi and can also be used on sub-hydrostatic wells.

The components of the 7Series subsea intervention system include:

  • Pressure Control Head (PCH)
  • Lubricator
  • Well Control Package
  • Circulation System
  • Subsea Shut-in Device

Browns Engineering has supplied operators with industry-leading service whenever and wherever needed. Whether riser or riser-less, we can assist in your operation. Our systems are flexible and can deploy from any vessel of opportunity. Available for use in any part of the world, our intervention systems can switch on the fly from riser-less to riser-based operations using our specialized tooling without resurfacing. With the Browns Engineering systems, we can successfully achieve your well intervention operations while providing cost savings and time efficiencies. Our subsea operations support live well intervention, P&A, and hydraulic well stimulation.
